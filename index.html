Transfers made easy

1
Log in or register

- Not registered? Create a profile or learn more.

2
Verify your profile

- Visit one of our Central locations with a valid government-issued ID to verify your identity in person.

3
Start your transfer

- Log in to your profile.
- Enter the destination country and the amount you'd like to send.
- Choose how you’d like to send money - via bank transfer or credit/debit card1.
- Choose the payout method: cash or direct to bank.

4
Enter your receiver's details

- If you’re sending directly to a bank account, you will need your receiver’s bank name, code, and account number 2.
- You can save your receiver’s details for convenient repeat transfers.

5
Pay and send

- Pay for your transfer.
- Check all the details before hitting send. You'll get a confirmation email with the Money Transfer Control Number (MTCN). Use it to track your transfer and share it with your receiver only.

Walk in to send money

1
Visit a Western Union agent location

- With over 6,100 locations 3 in Thailand, it’s easy to find one nearby.

2
Share your details

- Provide the agent with your first and surname and a valid government-issued ID.

3
Share your receiver’s details

- Provide the agent with your receiver’s first name and surname, the destination country, and the exact amount you wish to send.
- Choose the payout method: cash or direct to bank.

4
Pay and send

- Pay for your transfer via credit/debit card1 or cash.
- You’ll get a confirmation email with the Money Transfer Control Number (MTCN). Use it to track your transfer and share it with your receiver only.
